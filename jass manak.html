ABOUT  

Jass Manak Ali Khan is an Indian singer and songwriter. He is known for his work in the Punjabi music industry, and his songs have been streamed billions of times online. Manak has won numerous awards for his music, including a Brit Asia Award and a Punjabi Music Award. Manak was born in Ludhiana, Punjab, India, in 1995. He began his musical training at a young age, and he was influenced by his father, who was a singer. Manak made his debut as a playback singer in the 2013 film "Mel Karade Rabba." However, it was not until the release of the song "Patola" in 2016 that Manak achieved widespread recognition. The song became a massive hit, and it helped to launch Manak's career as a leading playback singer in the Punjabi music industry. Since then, Manak has sung in over 50 films, and he has recorded songs in several Indian languages. He has collaborated with some of the biggest names in the Punjabi music industry, including Gurinder Gill, Amrit Maan, and Diljit Dosanjh. Manak's songs are known for their catchy melodies and their relatable lyrics. He is also known for his ability to connect with his audience through his music. Manak's music has been praised by critics and fans alike, and he is considered to be one of the most talented singers in the Punjabi music industry today.
