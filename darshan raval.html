ABOUT  

Darshan Raval is an Indian singer, composer, and songwriter. He is known for his work in different languages including Hindi, Gujarati, Punjabi and Bengali. In 2014, he participated in the StarPlus music reality show, India's Raw Star, finishing as the first runner-up. Raval was born in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India, on October 18, 1994. He began his musical training at a young age, and he was influenced by his father, who was a singer. Raval made his debut as a playback singer in the 2014 film "Gujjubhai The Great." However, it was not until the release of the song "Ek Ladki Ko Dekha Toh Aisa Laga" in 2016 that Raval achieved widespread recognition. The song became a massive hit, and it helped to launch Raval's career as a leading playback singer in Bollywood. Since then, Raval has sung in over 50 films, and he has recorded songs in several Indian languages. He has collaborated with some of the biggest names in Bollywood, including Himesh Reshammiya, Amaal Mallik, and Tanishk Bagchi. Raval's songs are known for their soulful vocals and their catchy melodies. He is also known for his ability to convey a wide range of emotions through his singing. Raval's music has been praised by critics and fans alike, and he is considered to be one of the most talented singers in India today. Here are some additional facts about Darshan Raval: He is a self-taught musician. He is a big fan of Kishore Kumar and Lata Mangeshkar. He loves to travel and experience new cultures. He is currently working on his debut album.
