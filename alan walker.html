ABOUT  

Alan Walker is a Norwegian DJ and electronic dance music producer who rose to fame in 2015 with his single "Faded." The song became a global hit, reaching number one in over 14 countries and being certified platinum in 16 countries. Walker has since released several other successful singles, including "Sing Me to Sleep," "Alone," and "All Falls Down."
